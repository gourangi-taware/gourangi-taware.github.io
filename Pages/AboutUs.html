Who We Are

SV Engineering, an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Company,Chakan, Maharashtra, is the wholly owned Indian Company. SV Engineering is emerging EPC engaged in providing Process Engineering Solutions and Services, including Design Engineering, Procurement, Erection and Commissioning for the Ethanol Plant, Starch Plants, Dairy, Food, Chemical, Pharmaceutical, Beverages and Brewery industries.

The company provides solutions & services based on the best technologies acrross the globe with prime focus on product quality energy efficiency.

The company's Pollution Control Business is based on Zero Liquid Discharge providing combination of Biological Treatment, Filtration, evaporation, crystallization and drying to provide best operational efficiencies.

Mission

SV Engineering is committed to provide sustainable & environment friendly designs & EPC solutions in the field of Ethanol, Starch, Chemical & Pharma Industries.

We view ourselves as partner with our customers, our employee, our community & environment.
