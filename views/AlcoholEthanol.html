Alcohol/Fuel Ethanol

SV Engineering provide technologies and systems for manufacturing various grades of ethanol such as Rectified Spirit, Extra Neutral Alcohol and Fuel Ethanol from varied feed stocks such as Molasses, Grains, Cane Juice and Cassava. The processes involved in any typical ethanol manufacturing plant can be categorized into two parts namely Process plant systems & Effluent Treatment plant sytems:

Process/Plant Systems

  • Fermentation:To produce Fermented wash with specific alcohol concentration as the feed stock for the distillation process

  • Distillation: For producing Rectified Spirit and Extra Neutral Alcohol

    • Atmospheric Distillation

    • Multi pressure Distillation

    • Molecular Sieve Dehydration:For producing Ethnol

Effluent Treatment Plant Systems

(A)For Molasses based distillery

Bio Methanation Plant:For producing Bio Gas from waste.

Integrated and stand alone Evaporation:For reduction in effluent volumes.

Spent wash incineration boiler:For firing the concentrated spent wash along with carrier fuel to produce steam for the complex.

(B)For Grain Based Distillery

Decantation system:For seperating wet cake and Thin slops.

Integrated Evaporation: To recover process condensate & thick syrup which is further mixed with wet cake.

Dryer: To abtain high quality fibrous DDGS as a byproduct, thus increasing plant profitability in environment & economical ways.
